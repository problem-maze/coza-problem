Protection
The mark needs room to breathe
Clear space — keep at least the eye's own height free on every side. Nothing else enters that margin: no text, no edge, no other mark.
Minimum size — never below 14px digital / 6mm print. Below that the solve-path fuses and the mark reads as noise.
