Common questions
What happens after 5 sessions on Free?
The sessions reset every 24 hours. You're never locked out permanently — just paced. Most people find 5 sessions per day is plenty.
Is my conversation private?
On the Free plan, nothing is stored — conversations disappear when you close the session. On paid plans, your history is encrypted and only visible to you. We don't read it. Ever.
What is a "pattern insight"?
Every week, Problem reads across your sessions and identifies recurring themes — the same wall appearing in different shapes. It's not therapy. It's a map of your maze over time.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. No friction, no dark patterns, no "are you sure?" loops. One click to cancel, effective immediately.
Is this therapy?
No. Problem is not a medical or mental health service. If you're in crisis, please reach out to a professional. Problem is for the everyday fog — the decisions, the patterns, the things you can't quite name.
